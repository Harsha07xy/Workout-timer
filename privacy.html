Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

This Workout Timer app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, workout session data (stored locally)
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data and preferences for targeted advertising
  • Location Data: Approximate location for location-based advertisements (if permitted)

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Display relevant and personalized advertisements
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Analyze app usage patterns
  • Provide workout timer functionality

Data Storage

Your workout timer settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using browser local storage. We do not store personal information on our servers.

Third Party Services

This app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Please review Google's Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics: For app usage analytics (if applicable)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local data anytime through browser settings
  • Disable location services for the app
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any material changes.
